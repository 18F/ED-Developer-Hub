    My Brother's Keeper (MBK) APIs

    The U.S. Department of Education's My Brother’s Keeper APIs provide access to several datasets compiled by the My Brother's Keeper Task Force, who gathered indicators from a range of federal data sources to support analyses of important life outcomes for all youth, including boys and young men of color. Specifically, these APIs provide information on 18- to 24-year-olds by race/ethnicity and sex, including high school dropout rates; college enrollment and graduation rates; rates of disconnected youth (who are neither working nor in school); imprisonment rates; and labor force participation rates.

    You'll need an API key. Sign up for one here.

High School Dropout Rates

api.ed.gov/data/mbk-highschool-dropout?api_key=DEMO_KEY1

  • Overview
  • Queries
  • Fields

Percentage of 18- to 24-year-olds who have not completed high school by sex and race/ethnicity, 2000–2014.

?per_page= Select number of results per page.
?page= Select results page.
?sort=Columnx or ?sort=-Columnx Sort by Columnx.
/_index_number_ Return a single record.
?Columnx= Filter by Columnx.
?Columnx= Filter by Columnx and Columny.
?Columnx=[]&ColumnX= Filter by multiple values in Columnx.
