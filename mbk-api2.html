My Brother's Keeper APIs

    Introduction_

    To encourage analyses of important life outcomes for boys and young men of color (and their peers), the My Brother’s Keeper (MBK) Task Force compiled statistical indicators from relevant federal data sources and made them available for download. The Department's My Brother’s Keeper APIs provide additional access points to several of these indicators. Specifically, ED’s MBK APIs provide access to information on 18- to 24-year-olds by race/ethnicity and sex, including high school dropout rates; college enrollment and graduation rates; rates of disconnected youth (who are neither working nor in school); imprisonment rates; and labor force participation rates.
